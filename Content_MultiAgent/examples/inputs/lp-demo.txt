Demo: LP revision path - commercial landing page

Article type: LP
Demo path: revision path
Target audience: agency owners and marketing managers who need a repeatable content production workflow for service pages.

Source context:
ClearPath SEO Studio sells a managed SEO content operations service for small SaaS teams. The offer combines SEO brief creation, AI-assisted drafting, editorial QA, uniqueness checking, localization, and final package delivery. The service is positioned for teams that want faster content production but still need reviewable artifacts before publishing.

Offer details:
- Primary offer: monthly SEO content package for SaaS landing pages and blog articles.
- Main benefit: faster first drafts with documented QA gates.
- Proof points available for the demo: transparent workflow, reusable briefs, manual approval checkpoints, final Markdown and JSON package.
- Desired CTA: book a workflow audit.

Intentional revision-path cue:
The first draft should avoid unsupported performance claims. If a claim such as "cuts content cost by 70 percent" appears without evidence, editorial QA should route the job back for revision.

Key points to include:
- The landing page should explain who the service is for, what workflow artifacts are delivered, and why manual gates reduce risk.
- The copy should present the final package as an inspectable handoff for editors, SEO specialists, and developers.
- Localization should be available after English QA and uniqueness pass.

Constraints:
- Avoid invented metrics, client names, rankings, or revenue claims.
- Keep the CTA specific and non-aggressive.
- Use a commercial but controlled tone.
